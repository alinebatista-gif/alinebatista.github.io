FAQ

COMMON
questions.

Can I change my package after starting? →
Yes — packages can be upgraded, downgraded, or paused with 7 days notice at the end of each billing cycle. No long-term commitments required.
What if I need more hours in a month? →
Extra hours beyond your package are billed at your package rate. I'll always flag when we're approaching the limit so there are no surprises.
What tools and platforms do you use to communicate? →
I adapt to whatever works for you — email, Slack, WhatsApp, Notion, Trello, Asana. During onboarding we agree on one main channel so nothing gets lost.
Do you work with clients in Brazil or other countries? →
Yes. I work 100% remotely and am fully bilingual in English and Portuguese. I've worked across time zones and can adapt my hours for different markets.
How do payments work? →
Monthly packages are invoiced at the start of each cycle. On-demand hourly work is invoiced weekly. I accept Venmo, Zelle, PayPal, or bank transfer.
